About Dr.Biu

Since its introduction in 2017,MINI GT has emerged as a brand that sets new standards for 1/64 model cars. With its unique balance between price and quality, MINI GT has captured the hearts of enthusiasts and collectors alike, offering a unique blend of artistry and engineering that brings iconic automobiles to life in meticulous miniature form.

MINI GT celebrates the affection between humans and machines, condensing years of automotive history and craftsmanship into palm-sized miniatures. Whether it's the sweeping curves of a vintage classic or the aerodynamic silhouette of a modern race car, MINI GT ensures the design essence of each vehicle is captured, making each model not just a collectible but a story in itself.

For collectors and automotive enthusiasts, MINI GT is more than just a brand. It's a journey through time, a gallery of automotive excellence, and a showcase of miniature artistry all rolled into one.

Welcome to MINI GT, the wonderful world of 1:64.
